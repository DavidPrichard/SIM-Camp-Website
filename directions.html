Directions

The Transit Plaza on Wright Street is a block south of Atlgeld and served by the Yellow, Blue, Bronze, Brown, Silver, Navy and Illini bus routes. The Illini Union stop is in front of Altgeld and served by the Red, Green, Bronze, Gold, Teal, Silver, and Illini bus routes.

You can find more information from MTDhere.

There are metered parking spaces along Wright Street across from Altgeld Hall. We will also be able to provide parking permits. More information about parking will be available later this summer.

There are bike racks on the south side of Altgeld Hall.
